vidlingo

Bilingual subtitles + flashcards for any YouTube video, in a side panel.

vidlingo turns any YouTube video with captions into a set of bilingual sentence flashcards — right in a side panel next to the video, so you never have to switch tabs.

How it works

  1. Open any YouTube video that has captions.
  2. Click "Add to library." vidlingo reads the transcript and translates it using a provider you control.
  3. Review the deck: every sentence is a card with the original text, its translation, and tap-to-look-up definitions for individual words. Bookmark sentences or words you want to revisit, replay any segment at a slower speed, and jump back into the video from any card.

Learn in six languages

Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Spanish, French, and German are all supported as source or target languages, with dictionary-backed word lookup for each. Per-language dictionary data downloads on demand — nothing is bundled you don't use.

Your own translation provider, your choice

vidlingo never sends your video/caption data through vidlingo's own servers. Pick Chrome's built-in on-device translator (free, no key, nothing leaves your device) or any OpenAI-compatible LLM API endpoint you configure, with your own key stored only in this device's local storage.
